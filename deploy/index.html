Our Story

“We wanted to create something special to the community, creating memories and traditions. That’s what a family business is about” - Christina Seid (owner)

Chinatown Ice Cream Factory (CICF) has been creating delicious ice cream for almost four decades and is often referred to as an “unofficial NYC landmark”. CIFC continue its tradition of being a family run operation; now with a second generation.

What’s the secret to our delectable frozen products? The answer is simple: Ice cream making is in our genes.
